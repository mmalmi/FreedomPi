Slava Ukraini!

  • This is an open wifi hotspot that lets you communicate and share files with nearby people. If you move between multiple Slava Ukraini hotspots, your browser synchronizes messages between them. It's a public chat, so don't say anything confidential.
  • If Internet connection is available (hotspot plugged into ethernet), it is routed over Tor, which can bypass some censorship and protect the hotspot operator from liability.
  • The hotspot is broadcasting the Ukrainian national song on the radio frequency 95.9 MHz. Low power transmission is unregulated in many countries, but do check your local regulations. If you want more power, set up an antenna.
  • For private, non-anonymous, offline-first communication, check out Iris Messenger
